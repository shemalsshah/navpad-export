About Us

Founded by Shodhan Shah, Navpad Exports has been creating luxury handmade haute couture embroidery and fabrics since 1991.

Headquartered in Mumbai, India, we serve clients across Europe, Middle East, Asia, and the United States. Our legacy is built on precision craftsmanship, timeless artistry, and modern innovation.
